Operation:

With our Roller Shades, we empower you to select the operating method that best suits your customer’s lifestyle and preferences. Whether they opt for manual chain operation or the modern convenience of motorization, we offer you the freedom to customize their window covering experience.

Manual Clutch with child safety

For those who appreciate the tactile sensation of control, our manual chain operation provides a classic way to adjust your shades. Simply pull the chain to raise or lower the shades to your desired level, giving you a hands-on approach to light and privacy management. In compliance with 16 CFR 1260.

MOTORS

Experience the future of window shade control with our motorized option. Using a remote control or smartphone app, your customer can effortlessly operate shades with the touch of a button.

Cordless

The Cordless Roller Shades eliminate the need for cords or chains, reducing the risk of entanglement hazards for children and pets. This feature makes them a secure choice for homes with young family members.
